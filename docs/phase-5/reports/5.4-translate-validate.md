## Summary

Phase 5.4 wires Steps 5 (Translate) and 6 (Validate) of the new learning wizard.
A single `POST /api/toolbox-talks/{id}/translations/{code}/start-translation` endpoint
creates a `TranslationValidationRun` (flagged `IsNewWizard=true`) and enqueues the
existing `TranslationValidationJob`. The job was already responsible for all translation
and validation work; this phase adds a new entry-point that bypasses the old wizard's
session-relevance guard and fires workflow-state events on completion.
